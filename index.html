The Model

Every Classroom
Builds Something Real.

01

Learn & Work Mode

Industry engineers teach how they actually work — trim, BIW reinforcement, wiring harness, seating structures. No theory without practice.

02

Design + Engineering

Conceptual and industrial design merged with engineering. Students understand both the 'what' and the 'how'.

03

Graduation = A Real Product

Every batch designs a complete vehicle — technology to hardware. Not a rendering. A deliverable. Your name is on it.

04

Industry as Client

Companies bring real briefs. Students solve them. Companies get production-ready designs. Institution earns revenue and credibility.

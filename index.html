The Pipeline

Plant Early. Train Deep. Place High.

Stage 1

Awareness

10th, 11th, 12th

Introduce automotive & industrial design as a real career path. Friendly books. Simple language. Ignite curiosity before they choose engineering.

Stage 2

Training

3rd & Final Year Engineers

Parallel curriculum alongside regular studies. 3–5 students per domain. Real industry knowledge before they graduate. Not replacing — complementing.

Stage 3

Placement

Top Companies in India

Within 12 months of training. Tier-1 suppliers, OEMs, design consultancies. Placement rate is the only metric that matters.

A 12th grader who reads your book → joins engineering → finds your curriculum → gets trained → gets placed → comes back as instructor.

Curriculum & The Book

Two Tools That Don't Exist Yet.

  • Runs alongside regular engineering degree

  • Taught by real industry engineers

+
The Book
  • Written for 10th, 11th, 12th students

The Model

Every Classroom Builds Something Real.

01

Learn & Work Mode

Industry engineers teach how they actually work — trim, BIW reinforcement, wiring harness, seating structures. No theory without practice.

02

Design + Engineering

Conceptual and industrial design merged with engineering. Students understand both the 'what' and the 'how'.

03

Graduation = A Real Product

Every batch designs a complete vehicle — technology to hardware. Not a rendering. A deliverable. Your name is on it.

04

Industry as Client

Companies bring real briefs. Students solve them. Companies get production-ready designs. Institution earns revenue and credibility.
